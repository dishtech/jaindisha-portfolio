Six research-backed palettes for a therapy site

All muted (the #1 rule for therapy design) and temperature-balanced so colors actually mix. Same hero and words across all — judge only the colour. Click your favourite, or two to compare.

Why Tide & Sand felt off: a bold cool-cyan teal against warm yellow cream = opposite temperatures fighting. The fixes below either mute the teal, or pair blue/green with a temperature-matched neutral.
