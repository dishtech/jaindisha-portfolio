01 Overview

A calm, personal, artistic website for Disha Jain — a PsyD trainee, counsellor, and psychology mentor. The site is counselling-led: a visitor arriving in a vulnerable moment should feel understood, trust her, and book a paid counselling session in one unhurried scroll — with her psychology-mentoring offering presented as a clear secondary path.

Primary goal: visitor books and pays for a counselling session.
Secondary: route aspiring-psychologist visitors to her mentoring services; let her see bookings at a glance (admin).

Practitioner — Disha Jain (@psywithdish). PsyD (Doctorate of Psychology) trainee at NIEPVD, Dehradun · MSc Clinical Psychology · BA Psychology · 7+ years in the field. Tagline: "A guide for the mind, a companion for the heart." Existing profile: topmate.io/psywithdish · LinkedIn — source for the real content below.

02 Brand & tone

  • Feeling: peace, warmth, kindness, safety. "Being held," not clinical.
  • Voice: gentle, plain-spoken, human. No jargon, no hype, no emojis in UI.
  • Personality: warm-organic editorial. Distinctive and personal.
  • Name / brand: Disha Jain · handle psywithdish. No "Dr." (she is a trainee).
Trainee positioning — needs her sign-off. Disha is a PsyD trainee, not a licensed psychologist. Copy must read "PsyD trainee / counsellor" and never imply full licensure or use "Dr." Confirm exact credential wording with her and her supervisor / board (RCI) before content lock.

Typography

  • Display: Fraunces (serif, optical sizing) — headings, prices, quote. Italic for accent words (whole, yourself, Always).
  • Body / UI: Hanken Grotesk — paragraphs, nav, buttons, labels.
  • Responsive scale via clamp(); hero clamp(46px, 6.4vw, 94px).

Motion — GSAP in production, CSS in the mockup

  • One orchestrated hero load: staggered fade-rise (kicker → headline → lead → CTAs → figure).
  • Scroll-reveal fade-up per section (IntersectionObserver / GSAP ScrollTrigger).
  • Micro-interactions: card lift, button arrow nudge, soft shadow growth, slow floating hero blob.
  • Restraint: calm and subtle, never flashy.

Imagery & texture

  • Stock photography for now (Unsplash), swappable for real photos later. Warm, natural light.
  • Arched image frames, organic blurred blobs, film-grain overlay, soft gradient mesh.

04 Homepage structure

Emotional arc: feel understood → trust her → understand the process → choose → reassurance → act.

  1. Hero — headline "A gentle space to feel whole again.", tagline "A guide for the mind, a companion for the heart.", two CTAs (Book a session / How I work), arched portrait + floating "PsyD trainee · 7+ yrs" card.
  2. You're not alone — 3 feeling cards drawn from what she works with: Stressed & overthinking · Low self-esteem · At a crossroads (academic / career / relationships).
  3. About — portrait + real bio + credential chips: PsyD trainee (NIEPVD, Dehradun) · MSc Clinical Psychology · BA Psychology · 7+ years.
  4. How I work — her eclectic approach (Person-Centred · CBT · REBT · Psychoanalytic — "tailored to you, not one-size-fits-all"). 3 steps: Reach out → We meet → We continue.
  5. Sessions (counselling) — bookable; Cal.com + Razorpay live here:
    • Counselling Session — ₹599 · 40 min (featured)
    • Priority Support Session — ₹1,299 · 40 min
  6. For aspiring psychologists (secondary path) — a band routing students to her mentoring: Student Guidance (₹499–999) · Mock Interview prep (₹799) · Mock Interview series (₹1,890). CTA → mentoring booking.
  7. Testimonial — single serif quote on sage band. (See ethics note.)
  8. FAQ — confidentiality · online sessions · first-timer · rescheduling.
  9. Final CTA + footer — "Whenever you're ready, I'm here." Footer links: Topmate, LinkedIn, email.
Testimonial — ethics. Many codes prohibit soliciting testimonials from current clients. Use a past client with written consent, anonymised, or reframe — confirm with Disha.
Still to supply: real session format/durations (Topmate shows 40-min video — online-only?), testimonial(s) with consent, professional photos, contact email.

05 Booking → payment flow

  1. Choose session (from Sessions section)
  2. Pick a time + details — Cal.com booking widget, synced live to her calendar (no double-booking, auto reminders)
  3. Pay — Razorpay checkout (UPI / card / netbanking)
  4. Confirmation — slot held in Cal.com + her calendar; email sent
Scheduling — decided: Cal.com (calendar-synced from day one). Embedded and themed to the Sanctuary palette. Booking is confirmed only after the Razorpay payment is verified server-side, and the Cal.com booking ID is saved on the Firestore record.

06 Admin dashboard — /admin, behind login

  • Counters: Total sessions · Scheduled (upcoming) · Completed · Revenue (this month).
  • Bookings table: client, session type, date/time, status badge (Scheduled / Completed / Cancelled), amount.
  • Auth-gated; counts come from Firestore.

07 Architecture

Stack: Astro (SSR / hybrid + adapter) · Firebase (Firestore + Auth + Cloud Functions) · Razorpay · Cal.com (scheduling).

Browser (Astro pages + GSAP) /admin (Firebase Auth) │ │ ▼ ▼ Astro server — /api/create-order · /api/verify-payment · /api/bookings │ (holds the Razorpay SECRET key) ├──────────────► Firestore bookings { client, session, datetime, status, amount, calBookingId } ├──────────────► Firebase Auth (admin login) ├──────────────► Razorpay (order · checkout · signed callback) └──────────────► Cal.com (calendar-synced slot picker → her Google Calendar)

Security — non-negotiable

  • Razorpay secret key never reaches the browser (server env var only).
  • Payment signature verified server-side before a booking is marked paid — a faked client "success" is rejected.
  • Admin behind real auth, not a secret URL.
  • HTTPS everywhere; Firestore rules lock writes to the server and gate admin reads.
