Gentle answers

Things people often wonder.

Is everything confidential? +

Yes — completely. What you share stays between us, within the standard ethical and legal limits I'll always explain up front.

Online or in person — which is better? +

Both work beautifully. Online offers ease and comfort from home; in-person suits those who prefer a dedicated space. We can choose together.

What if I've never done therapy before? +

That's perfectly okay — most people haven't. There's nothing to prepare. We'll start gently and go at a pace that feels safe.

Can I reschedule a session? +

Of course. Life happens. You can reschedule up to 24 hours before with no fuss.
